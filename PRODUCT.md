# DeployLens Product Register

## Product

- Product: DeployLens
- Platform: responsive web application
- Primary user: an engineer investigating a live checkout incident under time pressure
- Job: turn one metric-drop question into a compact, evidence-backed incident explanation
- Positioning: ask why a metric moved; get the incident, not a paragraph

## Experience

- Personality: precise, calm, decisive
- Evidence comes before explanation; prose outside the incident result stays brief.
- Every filter must preserve the link between the finding, timeline, funnel, and segment.
- Progressive disclosure keeps the initial view legible on a laptop and a projector.
- Motion communicates state only and respects reduced-motion preferences.

## Anti-patterns

- Generic SaaS dashboard chrome or decorative AI chat
- Nested cards, ornamental gradients, and low-contrast data
- Raw telemetry in the browser or claims unsupported by visible evidence
- Color-only status, inaccessible controls, or layouts that collapse on narrow screens
